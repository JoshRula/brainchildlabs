Why RFID for Exhibit Houses

RFID outperforms manual tracking and barcodes, especially for the fast-paced world of exhibit management.

RFID vs. Manual Tracking and Barcodes

  • Manual tracking is time-consuming and error-prone.
  • Barcodes require line-of-sight and individual scanning.
  • RFID enables rapid bulk scanning without direct visibility.

Exhibit House Advantages

  • Fast-paced show environments
  • Complex booth re-use scenarios
  • Loss prevention and shrinkage tracking
